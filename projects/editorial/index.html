Casting the Invisible
2017

Casting The Invisible is an editorial design, in which I juxtaposed Beatrice Ward’s The Crystral Goblet with Matthew Butterick’s “Drowning the Crystal Goblet” and Rice Gallery’s exhibition description of Yasuaki Onishi’s Reverse of Volume RG, 2012. I gave different typographic treatments to the three texts to clarify the function of each in relation to another. I treated the editorial as an art book, featuring various photographs of Onishi’s installations.

❮ ❯

Adobe InDesign and Adobe Photoshop were used to create this project.
